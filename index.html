THE CHILD-FOUNDATION

The Child-Foundation runs schools for underprivileged children with the belief that education is the vehicle to help poor families out of poverty.
We aim to equip children with literacy and skills that can enable them to gain employment and bring financial relief to their families.
One unit of Donation to this program covers the cost of a uniforms, sports and craft activities for a child. A key part of retaining teachers has been to pay them good salaries as well as providing a supportive environment, good infrastructure and teaching aids. This is the highest cost in CDF's annual budget.
In addition, the non teaching staff is also mentored and most have been in service since their respective schools started. Most teachers in CDF have been with their schools since inception.
Through training, mentoring and assigning them additional responsibilities, teachers have gained confidence, upgraded their skills and several teachers have taken on the mantle of running their schools very efficiently.
